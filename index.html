About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2022 in Ernakulam, Kerala, T Cube Printing Solution is a growing name in the printing and packaging industry. We are dedicated to providing reliable and high-quality solutions that meet the needs of modern businesses. We specialize in a wide range of solutions including Food Packaging Boxes, Cake Boxes, 3-Ply and 5-Ply Corrugated Boxes, Custom Boxes, and Offset Printing services. With advanced offset printing machinery (112x82 cm, four-color printing), At T Cube, our mission goes beyond just delivering boxes. We focus on timely service, innovative designs, and customer satisfaction, making us a reliable partner for businesses across Kerala and beyond. Whether it’s protecting products or enhancing their presentation, we provide packaging solutions that leave a lasting impression.
